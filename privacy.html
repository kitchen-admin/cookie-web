Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

At Cookie Kitchen, during our pre-launch phase, we collect the following personal information when you sign up for our waitlist:

  • Email Address: Used to reserve your spot on our waitlist and send you future updates.

We do not proactively collect other personal details during the waitlist sign-up stage, though usage data (such as IP addresses and browser types via standard analytics) may be collected to help us improve website functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used primarily to:

  • Notify you when our meal planning service officially launches.
  • Send periodic updates regarding the development and features of Cookie Kitchen.
  • Analyze website traffic to improve our overall user experience correctly.

3. Data Sharing and Protection

We are fully committed to protecting your privacy. We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. Your data may be shared only with trusted third-party service providers (e.g., email distribution tools) who assist us in operating our site and distributing our newsletter, governed by strict confidentiality agreements.

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you submit your email address.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to unsubscribe from our waitlist and mailing lists at any time. Each email communication we send out will include a clear mechanism for unsubscribing. Subject to applicable laws, you also maintain the right to view or request deletion of any personal data we possess regarding you.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding how we process your personal data, or if you would like to exercise any of your privacy rights, please reach out via email at support@cookie.kitchen.
